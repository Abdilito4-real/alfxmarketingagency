Common Questions

How do I know this won't be a scam?

We completely understand your caution in the crypto space. We understand the skepticism Web3 has its fair share of bad actors. At Alf X, transparency and delivery are non-negotiable.

Every campaign we run is structured with clear deliverables and milestones. We often start with small, testable executions so new clients can verify results firsthand before scaling. We've built long-term relationships by consistently delivering, not overpromising.

How fast can I expect results?

At Alf X, everything we do is campaign-driven not just a service drop. Our packages (Basic, Standard, and Premium) are structured into layered campaigns that target both immediate traction and long-term impact.

For example, our Basic campaign focuses on quick exposure with listings and call posts, while our Premium campaign combines trending, mass DMs, watchlists, and Chinese lounge presence to build momentum from multiple angles. Some effects—like trending boosts or call traffic—are instant, while others—like custom DMs and Reddit waves—build gradually for sustained engagement.

Can I start with a small test?

Absolutely. We encourage first-time clients to test our services before committing to a larger push.

Whether it's a scaled-down version of the Basic package, a single trending campaign, article drop, or limited DM batch, we'll tailor a lite version of our service to prove our value and build confidence.

After the test, 83% of clients upgrade to full campaigns once they see our metrics-driven approach.

What's your typical response time?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For Telegram messages, we typically reply within a few hours.

We're active and responsive, most inquiries receive replies within minutes to an hour, depending on time zone differences.

Urgent campaign updates or escalations are always prioritized. We believe smooth communication is key to solid execution.

Do you work with all types of crypto projects?

We're open to all innovative Web3 ideas—whether you're launching a memecoin, utility token, NFT brand, or DeFi platform.

However, we carefully vet each project to ensure alignment with our values and to maintain the integrity of our network. If it's legit and you're serious about growth, we're ready to build.
